New Appointments are $250, follow up appointments are $150.

If you are using insurance, Dr. Buch is in network with Blue Cross Blue Shield, United Healthcare, Oscar, Cigna, Aetna and GHI/Emblem. However, plans differ and we will verify your benefits/copay. If you would like to go through private insurance, schedule a new appointment and choose the "Insurance" option. Enter your insurance information as directed, and schedule your appointment. We will verify your benefits prior to your appointment time.

*Additional costs: All calls the doctor must make to insurance companies for prior authorization are charged $100. Any detailed forms that patients request that the doctor fill out are charged $200. Simple notes or letters confirming a diagnosis or treatment are free.

*Cancellation fees: At Skypiatrist, we understand that life gets in the way and sometimes you can't keep an appointment, so we have a very liberal cancellation policy.There is no charge if you cancel at anytime before a scheduled appointment time so long as you notify Dr. Buch by email or cancel the appointment on the website. There is a $50 charge for missed follow up appointments without any notification, and $100 charge for missed new appointments without any notification.
