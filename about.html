Online Psychiatric Care
Because the best couch is the one at home!

Experience all the benefits of Telemedicine!

At Skypiatrist, we understand how frustrating and time-consuming it can be dealing with any type of physician appointment. You commute to a clinic and wait an unpredictable length of time to see the doctor for a visit that might be over in 10 minutes. What should be a short, simple appointment takes several hours out of your day.

Dr. Sandip Buch, a board-certified psychiatrist with more than 10 years in practice, has designed Skypiatrist.com to provide high quality psychiatric care, while maximizing each patient's convenience and privacy. Our visits are always on time. Your prescriptions are sent straight to your pharmacy- no need to drop off paper prescriptions and wait for them to be filled. Not only will you never be forced to wait, you will never be rushed out of an appointment, because if you still have questions/concerns at the end of your appointment, you can schedule another videochat or phone call with the doctor free of charge until all of your concerns have been answered. The doctor is also available by email to answer any questions. This level of privacy and convenience can't be matched by traditional practices.

Making better use of technology

At Skypiatrist, we use the convenience of modern technology to improve your psychiatric care. Through a secure, HIPAA compliant videochat built right into our website, we provide follow-up in the privacy of your own home, office, or whatever location you choose.
